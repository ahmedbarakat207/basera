=========================================
  HOW TO TEST CROSS-DEVICE MONITORING
=========================================

SETUP (one-time):
─────────────────
1. On Phone A — sign in as CHILD (child@basera.com / childpassword123)
2. On Phone B — sign in as PARENT (parent@basera.com / parentpassword123)

LINK CHILD TO PARENT:
─────────────────────
3. On Phone B (parent) — tap the 👤+ icon in the top-right of the AppBar
4. In the "Link Child Account" dialog:
     Child's Email:    child@basera.com
     Child's Password: childpassword123
5. Tap "Link Account" — you'll see the child appear in the dropdown selector

SIMULATE BROWSING (Phone A - Child):
─────────────────────────────────────
6. On Phone A, tap any of the preset chips:
     ✅ Safe sites:   Wikipedia, Duolingo, Khan Academy, Scratch
     ⚠️ Unsafe sites: Slots 🎰, Violent Games 💥
   Or type any URL in the field and tap "Go"

MONITOR IN REAL-TIME (Phone B - Parent):
─────────────────────────────────────────
7. On Phone B, the URLs appear in "History Feed" automatically (Firestore stream)
8. Tap "Analyze with Iris AI" to generate an AI safety report on those URLs
9. Tap the bell 🔔 icon to see flagged content alerts
10. Switch to "Safety Charts" tab to view risk trend graphs

=========================================
  SINGLE-DEVICE DEMO (Offline/Local)
=========================================
If Firebase is unavailable, the app falls back to local SQLite storage:
- Visits are saved locally on the device
- Switch between Parent/Child mode using the ⇄ icon in the AppBar
- Demo data is pre-populated automatically
